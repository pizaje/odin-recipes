Alfredo Rotini

Home

Description

A simple dish easily homemade and bringing the restaurant taste with you!

Ingredients

  • Rotini
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Small cubed steak (around a centimeter)
  • Bacon
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto
  • Mozarella Cheese

Recipe

  1. Boil a box of rotini
  2. Boil two chicken breasts
  3. Cook cubed meat on a flat pan at medium-high heat
  4. Airfry bacon for 10-15 minutes at 400 degrees
  5. Mix two tablespoons of olive oil, a quarter of a stick of butter, and a pot of alfredo sauce into a deep pan
  6. Pour 3/4 of a cup of mozarella cheese on the mix until it melts completely
  7. Add 2 tablespoons of Pesto (green or orange)
  8. Pour cubed meat into the alfredo mix
  9. Shred chicken after boiling
  10. Dump the bacon fat into the chicken
  11. Crush the bacon
  12. Add any combination you wish of the following ingredients to shredded chicken:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Crushed bacon
    • Oregano
    • Basil
    • Rosemary
    • Dill Weed
  13. Drain rotini, then turn off its stove, then put it back in the pot
  14. Dump alfredo mix and chicken mix into the pasta
  15. Mix thoroughly until homogenous. You can add green onions to the mix at the end
